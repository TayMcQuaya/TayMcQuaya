About Me

Hey there! I'm TayMcQuaya!

I'm a Turkish guy born in Germany, now living somewhere in Bali. I’ve dabbled in programming—enough to build some cool stuff using the latest AI tech, but I still don’t quite call myself a developer. Instead, I like to think of myself as a creator who’s always learning and experimenting.

Pixel art is a huge passion of mine. When I have free time, you’ll find me sketching away, bringing little characters and scenes to life one pixel at a time. My adventure with crypto started back in the day, but life as a busy teenager held me back. It wasn’t until 2020, during the COVID lockdowns, that I finally jumped on the crypto train. That’s when I got into NFTs and started investing in altcoins. Now, I’m diving into day trading, especially with those trending altcoins that keep things interesting.

I’ve been inspired by folks like Alex Becker and Elliot Trades, even taking part in their tricky yet fun riddle hunt to join Neotokyo. What is Neo Tokyo? Well, that’s a story for another time! Through the crypto community, I’ve met some amazing people (among them UnrealApe, who im endlessly grateful for supporting me and enabling my journey) and picked up a ton about finances, how the world really works, and how to constantly level up myself. I’m grinding hard every day to improve in every area of my life—it’s a never-ending, exciting journey to keep learning and "git gud."

I decided to drop out of college because I felt there was no point in sticking with boring studies when there was a whole world out there to explore and learn real, practical things that make life better. Speaking six languages is another perk—three of them I’m fluent in, one’s intermediate, and I can understand two more, even if I’ve forgotten how to speak them fully.

These days, I’m all about building projects, making money from what I create, and figuring out how to get better at everything I do. I’d love for you to check out my website, see what I’ve built so far, and stay tuned for even more exciting things on the way. Enjoy your own journey, and let’s ride this wave together!

P.S.: All artwork from the games i made are also made by me! There might be some websites/artworks which im not allowed to present yet. So stay tuned for when that changes!
